"CalorCalc_PersInfos"

Welcome to "CalorCalc_PersInfos", your personalized nutrition guidance tool designed to help you understand your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) and other important nutritional metrics.

Features

Calorie Stats
Estimate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) based on your age, gender, weight, height, activity level and body fat.
Ideal Weight And Body Mass
Estimate your ideal body weight and understand your Body Mass Index (BMI) to assess your overall health and fitness level.
Macronutrients
Learn about your recommended macronutrient distribution (carbohydrates, proteins, and fats) based on your goals (Cutting, Maintenance or Bulking).

About Us

Project Inspiration: Our project, "CalorCalc_PersInfos," was inspired by our shared passion for nutrition, health, and fitness. We wanted to create a personalized tool that helps individuals understand their Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE), ideal body weight, and macronutrient needs to achieve their health goals.

This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School., where we've combined our skills in web development to deliver a practical and informative application.

We are deeply interested in nutrition and its impact on overall well-being. Our goal is to empower users with knowledge and tools to make informed decisions about their health through technology.

Timeline: We started working on "CalorCalc_PersInfos" in April 2024, aiming to deliver a user-friendly and effective nutrition guidance tool by June 2024.
